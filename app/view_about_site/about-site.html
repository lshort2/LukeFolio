Why did you use BLANK?

I wanted to challenge myself and avoid using any of the tools I am normally accustomed to (bootstrap, django, AWS, etc).

I feel like sometimes it is good to have extra options in the toolbelt and just fun to have an idea of what other popular frameworks and tools are like.

Why did you not make LukeFolio earlier?

Truth be told, I am not a particularly big fan of portfolio sites, because I find it difficult to accurately portray so many experiences I have had.

I like having GitHub available with anyone in the world being able to dissect the code and commit history, if they so desire. Likewise, anyone can skip past repos or just read the readme.md and call it a day.
