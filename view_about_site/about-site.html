Why did you use BLANK?

I wanted to challenge myself and avoid using any of the tools I am normally accustomed to (bootstrap, django, AWS, etc).

I feel like sometimes it is good to have extra options in the toolbelt and just fun to have an idea of what other popular frameworks and tools are like.

What is the purpose of this site?

This site is intended to show different involvements in my life. I use the vague word "involvements" intentionally. Almost all the projects I have worked on are viewable from GitHub, so I am not so sure there would be much added value to listing them all out again here. This site is more so a way to share mention of some overarching things in my life that probably spanned months or even years.

It is not, however intended to be a full-fledged life story or documentary. More so, a way to show others some things that I have been involved in and if they are interested, they can ask me questions.
